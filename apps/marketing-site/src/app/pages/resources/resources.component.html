How we explain severity and common issues

We use three severities: High when a person may be blocked, Medium when the page is usable but frustrating, and Low for polish.

Common categories include text alternatives, color and contrast, page structure, and forms. The dashboard shows these definitions next to each issue so you know why it matters and how to fix it.
