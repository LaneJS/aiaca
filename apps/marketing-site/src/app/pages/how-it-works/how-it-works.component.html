How it Works

Accessibility engineering, simplified.

From a URL to a compliant site. Here is the technical breakdown of our process.

01

Deep Scan

We launch a headless browser instance to render your page exactly as a user sees it. We don't just parse HTML; we execute JavaScript, wait for hydration, and analyze the computed DOM.

Using industry-standard axe-core rules, we identify violations against WCAG 2.1 AA standards.

$ aaca scan https://mysite.com
> Launching headless browser...
> Hydrating DOM...
> Running axe-core audit...
✓ Found 12 violations
✓ 3 Critical, 5 Serious, 4 Minor
_
02

AI Analysis & Context

Raw error logs are confusing. Our AI engine analyzes the violating DOM nodes in context.

It understands that an image inside a button needs different alt text than a decorative background image. It generates semantic, human-readable fix suggestions tailored to your content.

analysis.json
{
  "violation": "image-alt",
  "node": "<img src='logo.png'>",
  "context": "inside <nav>",
  "ai_suggestion": {
    "fix": "Add alt='Company Home'",
    "reasoning": "Image links to root"
  }
}
03

Auto-Fix Injection

For common structural issues, you can install our lightweight script.

It automatically patches the DOM at runtime—adding missing ARIA labels, fixing focus outlines, and ensuring skip links exist—without you rewriting your codebase.

JS

<script src="aaca.js">

⬇
+ aria-label="Search"
+ tabindex="0"
+ alt="User Avatar"
