The Process

Compliance on Autopilot

A complete system to detect, fix, and prevent accessibility issues.

01

Automated Detection

Our scanner crawls your site using a headless browser, simulating real user interactions. It checks against 50+ WCAG 2.1 AA success criteria, identifying issues like low contrast, missing labels, and keyboard traps.

  • Checks dynamic content (SPAs)
  • Tests mobile viewports
  • Validates ARIA attributes
02

AI-Powered Remediation

Finding bugs is easy; fixing them is hard. Our AI analyzes the context of each error and generates specific code patches. It understands your DOM structure and suggests semantic fixes, not just overlays.

  • Context-aware alt text generation
  • Contrast ratio calculation
  • Semantic HTML structure fixes

03

Continuous Monitoring

Websites change. Content editors add images; developers ship new features. Our auto-fix script guards your live site, while scheduled scans alert you to regressions before they impact users.

  • Regression alerts via email
  • Historical score tracking
  • Exportable audit reports
