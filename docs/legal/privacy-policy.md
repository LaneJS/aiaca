## What we collect
- Minimal telemetry about scan requests (timestamp, URL host) to operate the free scanner.
- Authentication details for registered users (email, hashed password).

## What we avoid collecting
- We redact HTML snippets and form content from accessibility scan results by default.
- We do not store cookies or tracking IDs on the marketing site.

## Data retention
- Public scan request metadata retained for 30 days for abuse monitoring; content from pages is not persisted.
- Authenticated scan summaries retained for customer accounts; raw page contents are discarded after analysis.
