Questions

Everything you need to know

Does the free scan include AI suggestions?
Yes! We share a limited set of AI-backed suggestions on the first few issues to help you take action. Upgrade to Starter for unlimited AI suggestions across all detected issues.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. Plans are month-to-month with no hidden fees, contracts, or cancellation penalties. Cancel with one click from your dashboard.
Do you handle privacy-sensitive pages?
We avoid storing forms or logged-in data and document what we collect in the privacy policy. Your users' data stays private, and we only analyze the rendered HTML for accessibility compliance.
What happens after I run out of free scans?
The free scan is reusable on the same URL. If you want to scan additional pages or access advanced features like monitoring and auto-fix scripts, upgrade to Starter at any time.
How do the auto-fix scripts work?
Our auto-fix scripts identify common accessibility issues and provide automated code fixes you can apply directly to your codebase. This saves hours of manual remediation work for standard WCAG violations.
Is there a discount for annual billing?
Not yet, but we're working on annual plans with savings. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when they're available.
