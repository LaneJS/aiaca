Frequently Asked Questions

Does the free scan include AI suggestions?

We share a limited set of AI-backed suggestions on the first few critical issues to help you understand the value, but full remediation code is available in the Starter plan.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Our plans are month-to-month. You can cancel directly from your dashboard with one click, no questions asked.

How does the auto-fix script work?

You add a single line of JavaScript to your website's header. Our script automatically patches common accessibility issues (like missing labels or focus states) without altering your visual design.

Do you handle privacy-sensitive pages?

We scan only what is publicly visible. We do not submit forms or access data behind login screens unless you specifically configure an authenticated scan.
